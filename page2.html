Square Hunt

This App Academy Project is called "Square Hunt," a game in which two players click on the squares to get ten squares. Whoever gets to 10 first wins. Javascript, CSS, and HTML were used to build this game. However, most of our time was spent on incorporating "Socket.io" to be able to have a two-player game at the same time and keep information of who clicked ten squares first. To play click Here
